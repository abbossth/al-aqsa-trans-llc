1. SMS Consent Communication

SMS opt-in or phone numbers for the purpose of SMS are not being shared with any third party and affiliate company for marketing purposes. The information (Phone Numbers) obtained as part of the SMS consent process will not be shared with third parties for marketing purposes. Your phone number will only be used by Al Aqsa Auto Transport for the purposes outlined in these SMS Terms & Conditions.

2. Types of SMS Communications

If you have consented to receive text messages from Al Aqsa Auto Transport, you may receive messages related to:

  • Quote confirmations and updates
  • Booking confirmations and scheduling information
  • Pickup and delivery notifications
  • Shipment status updates and tracking information
  • Customer service communications
  • Important updates regarding your vehicle transport
  • Follow-up messages after service completion

3. Message Frequency

Our SMS message frequency is estimated to be 1-3 text messages per day during active service periods. Message frequency may vary based on your service status and communication needs. You will receive messages primarily during active quote requests, booking processes, and shipment transit periods.

4. Potential Fees for SMS Messaging

Please note that standard message and data rates may apply, depending on your carrier's pricing plan. These fees may vary if the message is sent domestically or internationally. Al Aqsa Auto Transport does not charge fees for sending SMS messages, but your mobile carrier may apply standard messaging rates according to your wireless plan.

5. Opt-In Method

You may opt-in to receive SMS messages from Al Aqsa Auto Transport in the following way:

  • By checking the SMS consent checkbox when submitting an online quote form on our website

6. Opt-Out Method

You may opt out of receiving SMS messages from Al Aqsa Auto Transport at any time by:

  • Texting "STOP" to the number from which you received the message
  • Emailing us at richard@alaqsatransllc.com with your phone number and request to opt out
  • Calling us at (619) 944-8954 and requesting to opt out
  • Replying "UNSUBSCRIBE" to any text message we send

After opting out, you will receive a confirmation message. You will no longer receive SMS messages from us unless you opt in again in the future. Opting out of SMS messages does not affect your ability to receive email or phone call communications from us.

7. Help

If you are experiencing any issues or need assistance with our SMS service, you can:

  • Text "HELP" to the number from which you received the message
  • Email us at richard@alaqsatransllc.com
  • Call us at (619) 944-8954
  • Visit our Privacy Policy and Terms and Conditions pages

Additional Options

If you do not wish to receive SMS messages, you can choose not to check the SMS consent box on our forms. You can still use our services without opting in to SMS communications. All essential service communications will be provided via email or phone call if you choose not to receive SMS messages.

8. Standard Messaging Disclosures

  • Message and data rates may apply.
  • You can opt out at any time by texting "STOP."
  • For assistance, text "HELP" or visit our Privacy Policy and Terms and Conditions pages.
  • Message frequency may vary based on service status and communication needs.
